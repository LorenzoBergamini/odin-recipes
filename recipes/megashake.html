The Megashake

Description

It's a milkshake. It's a meal. It's a Megashake! This is the ultimate shake to end all shakes. It's a meal in a glass. It's a dessert and a drink all in one. It's a Megashake!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 2 spoons of peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • blueberries (optional)

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Enjoy!

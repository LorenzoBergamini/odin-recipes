Omelette

Description

These omelettes are light and fluffy and great for a weekend brunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped ham
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper.
  2. In an 8-in. nonstick skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Add egg mixture. As eggs set, lift edges, letting uncooked portion flow underneath. When the eggs are set, spoon cheese, ham and onion on one side; fold other side over filling. Slide omelet onto a plate.
